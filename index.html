Gym Form Trainer

A real-time exercise coach that interprets body landmarks, evaluates movement, counts repetitions, and sends feedback to physical hardware.

Problem
Useful form feedback must arrive while the movement is happening.
Approach
Joint-angle rules and smoothed pose landmarks drive visual and Arduino feedback.
Built with
Python, OpenCV, MediaPipe, C, Arduino, serial communication
