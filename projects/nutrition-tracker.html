AI Nutrition Tracker App

Two ways to log a meal, one daily record. Whether an entry is typed in detail or parsed by AI from text, a photo, or a barcode, it passes through the same review step before it ever gets saved.

Role
Independent developer
Stack
TypeScript, React, Supabase, Vite/PWA
Problem
Traditional food logging takes too much manual entry
State
Active development, LLM meal entry in progress

Product screens

Logged foods, daily totals, and a coaching insight.

Two paths, one checkpoint

Detailed

Traditional entry

Add each food and edit the nutrition fields directly. Full manual control over exactly what gets logged.

Faster

AI-assisted entry

Describe a meal in text, or use a photo or barcode, so parsing fills in the fields instead of typing each one.

Both paths, same rule

Nothing saves until it is reviewed

Typed or AI-parsed, every entry lands on the same review screen first. Items are shown as editable fields for calories, protein, carbs, fat, sodium, and potassium, so anything that looks off gets fixed before it becomes part of the daily record, not after.
